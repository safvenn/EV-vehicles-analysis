## 🧠 The Business Problem  

India is rapidly transitioning toward electric mobility — but:  

- Which **states** are truly driving EV adoption?  
- Which **manufacturers** dominate the market?  
- Are **government incentives actually working?**  
- Where should a new EV company (*AtliQ Motors*) invest?

## 🔥 Key Insights  

- EV adoption is growing rapidly  
- 2-wheelers dominate the market  
- Few states contribute majority of sales  
- Infrastructure impacts adoption  
- Some states show declining trends  

---

## 💼 Business Recommendations  

- Focus on high-growth states  
- Invest in charging infrastructure  
- Target 2-wheeler segment  
- Use subsidies effectively  
- Expand in low-competition regions
